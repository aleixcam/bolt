Bolt

Music Player and Library Manager for lovers of music

Player

All that you would expect of a music player, is here.

With Bolt you can easily play your songs. Its user friendly design helps in jumping between songs, playing artists, randomizing all your songs and more!

Manager

Tired of songs without a defined artist? Tired of albums without a cover? We've got you covered!

The library manager built in Bolt allows you to edit your songs metadata and fill the sometimes frustrating lack of information in some songs.
